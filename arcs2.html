Story Arcs

The Black Swordsman Arc

The Black Swordsman Arc is the first story arc of Berserk and consists of chapters 1-8. Chronologically, it takes place between chapters 94 and 95 of the manga. It starts with the main character, Guts, getting into a fight with several thugs and killing them. He is thrown in jail for this. The "Snake Lord" of the town he is in hears about his power and comes to kill guts. The Snake Lord is an Apostle who serves a group of god-like figures called the Godhand. Guts defeats the Snake lord and asks him the wherabouts of the Godhand. Later, in another land, Guts makes his presence known to another Apostle known as The Count. Guts fights his way through The Count's castle and deals the killing blow to The Count, but before he died, he summoned the Godhand. Once the godhand was summoned, guts tried his hardest to kill one member named Femto, but was casually pushed away and the battle ended.

The Golden Age Arc

The Golden Age Arc is the second story arc of berserk and consists of chapters 9-94. At the beginning of this arc, Guts joins a mercenary band called The Band of The Hawk after the leader Griffith takes interest in him.. He spends three years working up the ranks in The Band of The Hawk while they gain fame and glory. At its height, Guts decides to quit the band and go on his own. After this, Griffith gets thrown in jail for a year. A year lateer guts reunites with the remnants of The Band of The Hawk. Griffith, almost dead in jail, summons the godhand who decide to let him join their ranks in exchange for the lives of all the members of The Band of The Hawk. This event is called "The Eclipse" where the members of the band were marked with a brand of sacrifice and hunted down and killed by demons. Guts survives, but still has the Brand of Sacrifice after he escapes.

The Conviction Arc

The conviction arc consists of chapters 95-176. It is divided into three man sections. The Lost Children Chapter, The Binding Chain CHapter, and The Birth Ceremony Chapter. In the Lost Children Chapter, Guts arrives in a village that has had many disappearances lately. There were supposedly elf-like creatures from the "misty valley" near the village abducting children. Attracted to Guts' brand of sacrifice, the elves mount an attack on the village. The villagers blamed guts for the attack, but he was able to escape the village after being ambushed by the Holy Iron Chain Knights. In the Binding Chain chapter, the Holy Iron Chain Knights catch up to Guts. Tired from his previous battle, he is in no shape to fight them. However, he manages to take their leader captive and escape from them. In the Birth Ceremony chapter, Guts is warned of a Mock Eclipse which is intended to bring Griffith back to the mortal world. Guts fights multiple apostles in this chapter, and it ends with Griffith's return.

The Millenium Falcon Arc

The Millenium Falcon Arc is the fourth story arc of Berserk and consists of chapters 177-307. It features Guts and the other main characters dealing with the aftermath of Griffith's reincarnation. Guts meets many new friends and he ends up with a crew consisting of some members of the Holy Iron Chain Knights, and elf, a witch, and his old friend from the Band of the Hawk. They end up going to a spirit tree in which a vary powerful witch helps them with their journey. Guts gets new armor called the Berserker armor which gives him many new powers, but is risky to use. They end up fight in in many battles, the most impoirtant of which was agains a demon army of Griffith's. The arc ends with them travelling by boat to the home of the elves, elfheim.

The Fansatia Arc

The Fantasia Arc is the fifth and current story arc of Berserk. It consists of chapters 308 and onwards. Guts and his crew face many challenges on their journey to Elfheim. They face a ghost ship and fight a sea god with the help of "Merrows" or mermaids. Once they make it to elfheim, they elf king helps restore the sanity of one of Guts' friends, and Guts meets the blacksmith who forged his berserker armor.
